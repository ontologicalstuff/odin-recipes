Mole Enchiladas

Description

Mole enchiladas are a Mexican favorite! The sweet & spicy chocolaty taste of ancient Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Mole sauce
  • Tortillas
  • Toppings
    • Sour Cream
    • Avocado
    • Cotija
    • Cilantro
    • Sliced radishes

Steps

  1. Preheat oeven to 350 degrees F
  2. arm corn tortillas on a hot skillet for a few seconds in each side, or fry them lightly in oil.
  3. Pour ¼ cup of mole sauce into the bottom of a 9×13” pan.
  4. Fill each corn tortilla with a handful of chicken and small sprinkle of cheese, then roll tightly, and place seam side down in the pan.
  5. Pour remaining mole sauce over the top.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven and top a drizzle of sour cream, a sprinkle of cotija cheese, cilantro and chopped avocado.
