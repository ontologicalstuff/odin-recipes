Flan

Description

Flan is a custard based dessert.

Ingredients

  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cream cheese
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Boil water, preheat oven
  2. Cook sugar to make caramel sauce
  3. Let cook to an amber color
  4. Pour into cake pan and coat bottom
  5. Make flan custard, mix cream cheese and sugar
  6. Blend in eggs then milks
  7. Pour custard over caramel
  8. Bake in water bath
  9. Cool
  10. Invert onto platter
  11. Slice and serve
